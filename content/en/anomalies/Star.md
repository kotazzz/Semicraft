# Star

**Star** is the physical manifestation of the [[en/anomalies/Virus|Virus]]. Not a player. According to lore, **destroyed by [[en/lore/C.Y.|C.Y.]]** (June 2026).

## Description

An anomaly without a solid body — an unnatural cloud of floating particles.

- **Bribery:** offered valuable resources, gained trust
- **Manipulation:** pushed players toward [[en/lore/Rituals|rituals]] → death, glitches, [[en/anomalies/Virus|Virus]]
- **Lore connection:** possessed **[[en/players/_Nameless_Ghost_|_Nameless_Ghost_]]**; through **[[en/players/SlimeyPretty|SlimeyPretty]]** led him into [[en/lore/The Void|The Void]]
