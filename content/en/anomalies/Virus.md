# The Virus

A specific anomalous **illness** affecting Semicraft players. Physical manifestation — **[[en/anomalies/Star|Star]]**.

## Description

Progresses through **multiple stages**. Effects of the final stage are unknown.

## Protection

- Stay near **pumpkins** or wear a carved pumpkin on your head
- Vietnamese sign: `BÍ NGÔ SẼ BẢO VỆ BẠN` — *"Staying near a pumpkin will protect you!"*
