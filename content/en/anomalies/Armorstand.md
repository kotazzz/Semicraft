# Armor Stand

A peaceful anomalous entity on the Semicraft server.

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |

| Threat | Low |

## Behavior

- Moves when players are not looking
- Leaves **wooden signs** with hints
- Helps with navigation and hidden passages
