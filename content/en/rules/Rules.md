# Semicraft Project Rules

Rules for the Minecraft game server and official Discord community.

## Game Server Rules

Match the info board at spawn:

1. **Don't destroy other people's buildings or steal from them.** Griefing is forbidden.
2. **Don't be rude to other players.** Respect all participants.
3. **Don't kill other players.** Unauthorized PvP is forbidden.
4. **Cheats are forbidden** (*X-ray and killaura are not allowed*), including X-Ray and Killaura.
5. **Bug abuse is forbidden** (*Bug abuse is not allowed*). Report vulnerabilities to admins.
6. **Join the army of cats** (*Join the army of cats*). Lore directive from [[en/players/HanoiHaze|HanoiHaze]].

## Discord Rules

- **Respect:** no insults, threats, discrimination, or toxicity.
- **No spam:** don't duplicate messages or abuse CAPS LOCK.
- **Off-topic:** ARG, terminal logs, and lore — research channels only.
- **No advertising:** other projects and servers — admin approval only.
- **Discord ToS:** follow platform rules.

## Enforcement

Admins may apply measures from warnings to permanent whitelist removal.
