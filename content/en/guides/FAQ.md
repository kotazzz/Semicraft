# Semicraft Connection FAQ

Solutions for common problems when trying to join the server.

## Problems and Solutions

### Error: "Can't connect to server"

- **Cause:** server maintenance, config update, or plugin update in progress.
- **What to do:** wait for maintenance to finish. Status in [Discord channel](https://discord.gg/9E3x6EbcA8).

### Error: "You are not whitelisted"

- **Cause:** your nickname is not on the whitelist.
- **What to do:** email [mahazeviet@gmail.com](mailto:mahazeviet@gmail.com) with your exact in-game nickname and whitelist request.

### "You are not whitelisted" but I played before

- **Cause:** likely maintenance (backup restore, auth restart).
- **What to do:** no need to reapply — watch Discord for status.
